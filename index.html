Founding Mentors

創始導師團隊

Led by veterans from top global asset management and strategic investment backgrounds, we bring elite real-world expertise to teenage education. 我們的導師團隊由來自全球頂級資產管理公司和知名企業的專家組成，致力於將一線實戰智慧轉化為青少年的成長動力。

Ji Yuanyuan

創始人：季苑苑

Ex-Global Top Asset Management Portfolio Manager 前全球頂級資產管理公司投資經理

Founder Ji Yuanyuan brings more than a decade of impressive experience from her career in investing. Her time studying economics and finance at the University of Hong Kong gave her a rock-solid foundation, while managing portfolios at top global asset managers sharpened her investment skills through real-world challenges.

Over those years, she's travelled far and wide, averaging visits to over a hundred companies and their founders annually. Through those in-depth, one-on-one conversations, she's seen firsthand the huge impact that great entrepreneurs and leaders have on people and society. As a mom of two, she's come to truly value the power of ongoing learning and practice in her own journey—and she's eager to pass on those lessons to others. That's how DripMind came to life: she wants to share the entrepreneurial spirit she's embraced with young dreamers.

創始人季苑苑擁有十餘年資深的投資實務經驗。她在香港大學經濟金融專業的學習為其打下了堅實的理論基礎，而在全球頂級資產管理公司擔任投資經理的歷練，更讓她在真實世界的挑戰中磨礪出敏銳的投資眼光。

多年來，她足跡遍布各地，年均走訪上百家企業及其創始人。透過深度的一對一交流，她親身見證了卓越企業家與領導者對個人及社會產生的巨大影響力。作為兩個孩子的母親，她在自己的成長歷程中深刻體會到持續學習與實踐的力量，並渴望將這些寶貴經驗傳承下去。這正是水滴智行（DripMind）誕生的初衷：她希望與年輕的夢想家們分享她所尊崇的企業家精神。

Gu Silun

聯合創始人：顧思倫

Cross-industry strategy specialist 橫跨戰投、零售與海外業務專家

Co-founder Gu Silun has consistently pushed beyond conventional boundaries—from a finance professional at global asset management firms to a highly versatile leader at Li Auto across strategic investments, overseas business, and frontline retail operations.

Inspired by seeing students abroad shine in the fields they love, she came to believe education should not be a standardized product but a lifelong journey of understanding the world outwardly and exploring the self inwardly, which led her to co-found DripMind to help children shorten the trial-and-error path, encouraging them to experiment boldly, and to connect outstanding entrepreneurs across industries so the real-world spirit of execution and positive energy can be passed on to the next generation.

聯合創始人顧思倫始終致力於打破傳統邊界——從全球資產管理公司的金融精英，到理想汽車橫跨戰略投資、海外業務與一線零售運營的跨界領導者。

受到海外學子在熱愛領域發光發熱的啟發，她深信教育不應是標準化的產品，而是一場向外認知世界、向內探索自我的終身旅程。這促使她共同創立了水滴智行，旨在幫助孩子們縮短摸索的路徑，鼓勵他們大膽嘗試，並連結各行業優秀的企業家，將真實世界的執行力與正能量傳遞給下一代。
